## üß© Project Overview

This project aims to utilize **Salesforce** to streamline the **collection and distribution of leftover food** from donors to the needy.  
By leveraging Salesforce‚Äôs automation, data management, and reporting capabilities, the system ensures **efficiency**, **transparency**, and **accountability** throughout the donation process.  

The project automates key tasks such as **volunteer assignment**, **donation tracking**, and **report generation**, reducing manual work and ensuring that food reaches the right people on time.  

---

## üéØ Problem Statement

Every day, large quantities of food go to waste due to poor coordination between food donors (hotels, events, households) and NGOs or volunteers who could distribute it to the needy.  
There is a **lack of a centralized system** to collect, organize, and manage these donations efficiently.

---

## üí° Proposed Solution

Using **Salesforce**, this system connects **Donors**, **Volunteers**, and **NGOs** through a single automated platform.  

- Donors can log leftover food details.  
- Volunteers receive automated collection tasks.  
- NGOs monitor and report on food distribution.  
- Admins oversee all operations through dashboards.  

This ensures faster response times, automation, and transparency across all stakeholders.
